FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How is GenZrix different from an agency?
Agencies charge premium rates and often deprioritize smaller clients. We're a managed execution company where vetted Gen Z talent works on real projects under proper accountability. You get quality at a fraction of agency cost, with single-point accountability.
How do you ensure the quality of work?
We recruit from top institutions and organize teams by domain expertise. Every team member has their reputation and portfolio on the line — this isn't a training ground, it's their actual professional work. We maintain internal review processes before delivery.
What's a typical project timeline?
Brand identity: 4–6 weeks. Website: 6–10 weeks. Marketing campaign: 8–12 weeks. Event execution: varies by scale. We scope every project individually in our discovery call and give you a clear timeline before committing.
What does onboarding look like?
Discovery call → proposal with scope, timeline, and investment → kickoff meeting with the assigned team → weekly progress updates. You get a dedicated contact who handles everything. No hunting down multiple vendors.
Can you handle multiple services at the same time?
Yes — and this is exactly our advantage. We run design, development, marketing, and content in parallel under unified briefing and coordination. No communication gaps between disciplines. That's the GenZrix model.
Do you take rush projects?
Yes. We can mobilize quickly for time-sensitive projects. Rush projects come with an expedited rate but we always prioritize delivery quality. Contact us and we'll figure out what's possible.
